Hover the boxes for description

  • Intelligent brick

    Z-translates the sample chamber and adjusts the position of the Illumination Unit so that the light sheet is always in the image plage of the detection lens. Triggers the laser(s) / camera(s) to acquire images at each Z step.
  • Electronic board

    Powers lasers and adapts lasers / cameras triggers.

  • Laser beam

    Laser source to form the array of light sheets.

  • Glass Tube

    Spreads collimated laser beams into broad cones.

  • Laser Wheel

    Controls the lateral position of the moving laser so as to align it with the fixed laser.

  • Cylindrical lens

    Focuses the light sheet at the sample position to a width of approximately 10 microns.

  • Sample Chamber

    The sample is embedded in an agar cylinder held in a quartz chamber. The light sheet position is adjusted thinnest at sample, optimizing optical sectioning.

  • Sample Rotation

    Rotates the sample through paired magnets (PM), motor M3 and a mechanism (RM) allowing the Z-translation of the chamber.

  • Motor M2

    Z-translates the ilumination chamber through a chain of gears.

  • Motor M1

    Z-translates the sample chamber.

  • Zoom Lens

    Forms an image of the fluoresence emitted in the light sheet plane on the camera.

  • CMOS Camera

    Collects the fluorescent light emitted in the imaging plane.

  • Filter Wheel

    Holds the emission filter attenuating light coming from the lasers.
